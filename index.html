Into Kilmainham: A VR Story

The player controls an unnamed character who finds themselves drawn into Kilmainham jail one night, and must find a way to escape. Echoes of the past still linger in the jail, and the player encounters the ghosts of various inmates and staff from the prison’s history. The player must listen to their stories and insights in order to solve puzzles and advance through the jail towards freedom. The game will cover three broad areas of the prison’s history: its early days from 1790 to 1850, including the Great Famine; the experimental expansion of the prison in the Victorian era; and finally the prison’s political significance in the wake of the 1916 Rising. There is a puzzle for each section of the game, which is thematically linked to the relevant time period. The game will provide the player with various insights into the history of the jail, however it does not have an explicitly educational purpose; it is rather intended to be an immersive and entertaining experience, using cutting edge technology to give players a glimpse into the past.
